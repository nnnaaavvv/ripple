Our Mission

The Ripple Project empowers young people to become environmental leaders by tackling the critical issue of paper waste through education, innovation, and community action.

Education

We teach sustainable practices and the environmental impact of paper waste through workshops and school programs.

Innovation

We develop creative solutions for paper reduction and recycling through youth-led design challenges.

Community Action

We organize local clean-ups, recycling drives, and advocacy campaigns to create tangible change.
